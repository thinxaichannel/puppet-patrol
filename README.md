# Puppet Patrol: Monster Meadow

A cute two-lane arcade defense game. Command a squad of felt puppets, shoot buff gates to grow your army, flip curse gates before they reach you, and survive monster waves and bosses.

Play: https://thinxaichannel.github.io/puppet-patrol/

How to play: drag/tap or use arrow keys to switch lanes. Puppets fire automatically. Green gates grow as you shoot them - walk through to collect. Red gates start negative - shoot them positive or they curse your army wherever you are. Beat the boss to clear each level.

Built with vanilla HTML5 Canvas + WebAudio. No dependencies. Made with Claude (Fable 5).
